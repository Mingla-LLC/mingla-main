> app. The Business app must serve that positioning, not contradict it.

---

## 0. Document Conventions

- All claims here must tie to a real user, a real job, or a deliberate strategic bet.
- No speculative product fiction. If a section is undecided, it stays marked **TBD** with
  the open question written down.
- Every feature in MVP v1 must declare: who uses it, what job it does, what success looks
  like, and what we will measure.
- Cross-domain rule: every business-side feature is checked against impact on the consumer
  app, the shared Supabase schema, and the admin dashboard.
