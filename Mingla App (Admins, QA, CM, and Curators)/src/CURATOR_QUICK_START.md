# Curator Dashboard - Quick Start Guide

## Sign In as Curator

1. **Navigate to Sign-In Page**
   - App will load to the sign-in page

2. **Select Curator Role**
   - Click on "Curator" role card

3. **Enter Credentials**
   - Email: any email (e.g., `curator@mingla.com`)
   - Password: any password
   - Click "Sign In"

4. **Dashboard Loads**
   - You'll be taken directly to the Overview page

## Navigate the Dashboard

### Desktop Navigation
- Use the left sidebar to navigate between sections
- Active section is highlighted in orange gradient
- Logo is prominently displayed at top (80px/96px)
- Profile card shows your curator info
- Sign out button at bottom

### Mobile Navigation
- Tap menu icon (☰) in top right
- Mobile menu slides in from right
- Tap any section to navigate
- Menu closes automatically
- Bottom of menu has sign out button

## Explore Each Section

### 1. Overview (Home)
**What You'll See:**
- 4 key stat cards at the top
- Quick action buttons (Create Card, Manage Businesses, View Messages, View Analytics)
- Card status overview (Live, In Review, Drafts)
- Performance highlights grid
- Earnings summary card

**Try This:**
- Click "Create Card" to open card creator modal
- Click "View Analytics" to jump to analytics section
- Review your card status breakdown

### 2. Messages
**What You'll See:**
- Full messaging interface with header
- List of business partner conversations
- "New Message" button in header

**Try This:**
- Browse existing message threads
- Click on a conversation to view details
- Note the clean, organized layout

### 3. My Cards
**What You'll See:**
- Stats row (Total, Live, In Review, Drafts)
- Filter bar (Search, Status, Category, View Mode)
- Card grid/list with preview images
- Status badges on each card

**Try This:**
- Toggle between grid and list view
- Filter by status (e.g., "Live" only)
- Search for a card by name
- Click "Preview" on a live card
- Click "Edit" to modify a card
- Try creating a new card with the "Create Card" button

### 4. My Businesses
**What You'll See:**
- Business management interface
- List of businesses you collaborate with
- "Add Business" button in header

**Try This:**
- Review your business partnerships
- Click "Add Business" to onboard a new business
- View commission rates for each business

### 5. Earnings
**What You'll See:**
- 4 key financial metrics
- Recent transactions list
- Earnings by business breakdown
- Performance progress bars
- Payout information card

**Try This:**
- Review your total earnings
- Check pending payout amount and date
- Browse recent transactions
- See which businesses generate most revenue
- Try the timeframe dropdown (7d, 30d, 90d, all)
- Click "Export" to download earnings report

### 6. Analytics
**What You'll See:**
- 4 key performance metrics
- Performance overview bar chart
- Engagement metrics with progress bars
- Top performing card showcase
- Insights cards at bottom

**Try This:**
- Change timeframe (7d, 30d, 90d, all time)
- Hover over chart bars to see details
- Review engagement rate and conversion rate
- Check your top performing experience
- Read the insights recommendations
- Click "Export Report" button

### 7. Support
**What You'll See:**
- 3 contact option cards (Live Chat, Email, Submit Ticket)
- Search bar for documentation
- 7 FAQ sections with expand/collapse
- Additional resources grid

**Try This:**
- **Search Documentation:**
  - Type "commission" in search
  - See matching FAQ questions highlight
  
- **Browse FAQ Sections:**
  - Click on "Getting Started as a Curator"
  - Click a question to expand answer
  - Read the comprehensive documentation
  - Try different sections (Earnings, Business Collaborations, etc.)
  
- **Test Contact Options:**
  - Click "Start Chat" for live chat
  - Note email support availability
  - Try "Create Ticket" button

### 8. Settings
**What You'll See:**
- Left sidebar with 5 settings sections
- Main content area for selected section
- Save buttons for each section

**Try This:**
- **Profile:**
  - Update name and bio
  - Add location and website
  - Upload profile picture
  - Click "Save Changes"
  
- **Account:**
  - View email address
  - Change password section
  - Note password requirements
  
- **Notifications:**
  - Toggle email notifications on/off
  - Control push notification preferences
  - Granular control over each type
  
- **Payout Settings:**
  - Review current payment method
  - See next payout schedule
  - Note tax information prompt
  
- **Privacy & Security:**
  - Control profile visibility
  - Toggle privacy preferences
  - Access 2FA setup
  - View danger zone (delete account)

## Troubleshooting

### Card Not Appearing?
- Check filter settings (status, category)
- Clear search query
- Verify card was saved correctly
- Refresh the page

### Earnings Not Showing?
- Check timeframe filter
- Verify transactions exist
- Try "All time" timeframe
- Refresh the page

### Support Search Not Working?
- Clear search input and try again
- Check spelling
- Try broader terms
- Browse FAQ sections manually

### Mobile Menu Stuck?
- Tap outside menu to close
- Refresh the page
- Check browser compatibility
